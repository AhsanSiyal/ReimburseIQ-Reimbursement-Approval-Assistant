# Company Reimbursement Policy Rulebook (v1.0)

## 0. Metadata
- Policy Owner: Finance
- Effective Date: 2026-01-01
- Applies To: All employees and contractors unless stated otherwise
- Baseline Currency: EUR

## 1. General Eligibility
R-GEN-001: Expenses must be business-related, reasonable, and necessary.
R-GEN-002: Expenses must be submitted within 30 calendar days of the expense date.
R-GEN-003: Personal expenses are not reimbursable.
R-GEN-004: Duplicate submissions are not reimbursable.

## 2. Receipts & Documentation
R-DOC-001: Receipt required for any single line >= 25 EUR (or local equivalent).
R-DOC-002: Lodging requires itemized hotel invoice (room rate, taxes, dates).
R-DOC-003: Airfare/rail requires proof of payment + itinerary.
R-DOC-004: Client entertainment requires itemized receipt regardless of amount.
R-DOC-005: Missing receipt requires Missing Receipt Declaration.

## 3. Approval Matrix
R-APP-001: Direct manager approval required for all reimbursements.
R-APP-002: Any single line > 300 EUR requires Department Head approval.
R-APP-003: Total claim > 1,000 EUR requires Finance approval.
R-APP-004: Client entertainment requires Manager + Finance approval.

## 4. Meals
R-MEA-001: Reimbursable when traveling or working outside normal location for business.
R-MEA-002: Caps per person: Breakfast 15 EUR, Lunch 25 EUR, Dinner 40 EUR.
R-MEA-003: Alcohol not reimbursable unless client entertainment with approvals.

## 5. Lodging
R-LOD-001: Nightly room rate cap (excl. taxes): 180 EUR (standard cities).
R-LOD-002: High-cost cities may exceed cap only with manager pre-approval attached.
R-LOD-003: Non-reimbursable: minibar, spa, movies.

## 6. Transport
R-TRN-001: Use public transport where feasible and safe.
R-TRN-002: Taxi/ride-hailing reimbursable when time-critical or no safe public option.
R-TRN-003: Receipt required for taxi/ride-hailing >= 25 EUR.

## 7. Mileage
R-MIL-001: Reimbursed at 0.42 EUR per km.
R-MIL-002: Must include start/end, distance, date, business purpose.
R-MIL-003: Commuting to normal office is not reimbursable.

## 8. Training
R-TRN-101: Training/conferences require pre-approval before purchase.
R-TRN-102: Attach invoice and agenda where available.
